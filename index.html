The Seven Wonders of the Modern World

An immersive journey to the most spectacular architectural marvels of our time.

The Great Wall of China

History

Built from the 7th century BC onwards, this series of fortifications was designed to protect Chinese states against nomadic raids. It's a testament to centuries of engineering and perseverance.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-November) offer pleasant weather and stunning views.

Location

The Colosseum

History

Completed in AD 80, this grand amphitheater hosted epic gladiatorial contests and public spectacles, symbolizing the power and drama of Imperial Rome.

Best Time to Visit

Visit during shoulder seasons (April-June, Sept-Oct) to avoid extreme heat and crowds.

Location

Christ the Redeemer

History

An icon of Art Deco, this colossal statue of Jesus Christ was completed in 1931, watching over Rio de Janeiro from the summit of Corcovado Mountain as a symbol of peace.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from December to March offers the clearest views and best weather for photos.

Location

Chichen Itza

History

This ancient Mayan city thrived from around 600 A.D. to the 1200s. The massive step-pyramid, El Castillo, demonstrates the Mayans' advanced astronomical skills.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from November to April is ideal. Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds.

Location

Petra

History

The capital of the Nabataean Kingdom, dating to around 300 B.C. Petra is famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system, earning it the nickname "Rose City".

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) offer the most pleasant weather for exploring.

Location

Taj Mahal

History

Commissioned in 1632 by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this ivory-white marble mausoleum is a timeless symbol of love and a jewel of Muslim art in India.

Best Time to Visit

October to March provides cool, pleasant weather. It is closed on Fridays.

Location

Machu Picchu

History

This Incan citadel, built in the 15th century, is renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone walls. It was abandoned a century later and remained unknown to the outside world until 1911.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from April to October is the best time for clear skies and hiking.
